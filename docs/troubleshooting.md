# ❓ Troubleshooting & FAQ

---

### **Why can't I log in?**
Check:
- Email & password
- Internet connection  
- If you previously deleted app data  

---

### **My PDF is not summarizing.**
Possible causes:
- Unsupported file format  
- Very scanned/low-quality documents  
- File larger than 20–40MB  

---

### **Why does the app ask for storage permission?**
To allow:
- Uploading PDFs  
- Saving audio files  
- Loading lecture recordings  

---

### **Why is the app loading slowly?**
Your network speed or cached resources.  
Try restarting the app.
